On this page you'll find links to sites on the net, where you can get a free e-mail address. There are 3 kinds of e-mail addresses:

  • An address where you can read and send mail with software like Eudora or Netscape Mail.
  • An address that forwards your e-mail to another address. This could be very usefull if you would like to have an address that is much easier to remember than your own. You can only receive mail.
  • An address where you can read and send mail if you go the website of the company.
